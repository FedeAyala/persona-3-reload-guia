## Episode Aigis

### Episode Aigis - Part 2

**Ptolomea**

Persona 3 Reload, Ptolomea B1-B8, Hard - YouTube

Tap to unmute

[Persona 3 Reload, Ptolomea B1-B8, Hard](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IrKnvq1OXkE) [Ten Square](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCCmfMTH_CHvv6AoAFqDMUqQ)

Ten Square191 subscribers

[Watch on](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IrKnvq1OXkE)

> Golden Plates and Red Wall Fragment drops from mobs behind the Monad Doors in this block, if you don't get them this time, you will have chances in upcoming blocksThe Greedy Shadow drops the Gold Medal in this block, but catching it is a matter of luck. If you're having bad luck catching it, Fuuka eventually learns Tartarus Scan at level 70. You can then return to this block to get the Gold Medal.

When you're ready, head for the door called Ptolomea and enter. Make your way down to B8 (Teleporter is on B7) to find the next gatekeeper, after defeating it you'll get the Chariot Major Arcana. Head to your right and open the chests, then give Aristocracy to Mitsuru, before using the door to return to the Desert of Doors.

> You might be able to hand in quests 29, 33 and 34. If you managed to get a Gold Medal, Golden Plates or Red Wall Fragment.

Hand in quest 9 to Elizabeth, which unlocks:

- 10\. Bring me Circe's Bow
- 38\. Bring me Shackles of Madness

You might be able to hand in quest 38, Shackles of Madness also drops from the mobs behind the Monad Doors as well.

Return to the dorm for a cutscene, afterwards you will unlock:

- 2x Random party members second Theurgies

Read a book with Akihiko

- Characteristics Unlocked: Buff Amp, greatly increases the effects of buffs on self

(Increases the effects of buffs on self by 30%)

Head up to the 3F and spend time with Mitsuru

- Characteristics Unlocked: Ailment Surge, greatly raises critical rate on foes with ailments

(Raises party's critical rate by 15% on foes with ailments)

Head to Paulownia Mall and speak to the Flower Shop Lady, to receive your harvest, then plant whatever you like.

Farm until you have 50-80k.

> Don't register Jatayu if it has dropped its heart item, otherwise you won't be able to summon it back and have it drop its heart item again.

> Due to rng nature of new personas appearing in shuffle times, this may happen for you sooner or later than here.

Enter the velvet room and register your personas. If you've been collecting new personas from shuffle times, you can get another discount on summoning personas, need to reach 50% in Episode Aigis compendium to receive.

Then fuse:

> Both of these fusions are for quests, so only the skill needed for the quest matters, you can use personas from either compendium.
>
> **If using DLC Personas**
>
> | **Fusion Recipe** | **Skills to Pass on** | **Skills to Keep** |
> | --- | --- | --- |
> | Orobas and Belpheor<br>Result: Titania | **Magarula** Skill needed for quest | N/A |
> | Narcissus and Jatayu<br>Result: Succubus | **Charm Boost** Skill Needed for quest | N/A |
>
> **If not using DLC Personas**
>
> | **Fusion Recipe** | **Skills to Pass on** | **Skills to Keep** |
> | --- | --- | --- |
> | Orobas and King Frost<br>Result: Titania | **Magarula** Skill needed for quest | N/A |
> | Narcissus and Jatayu<br>Result: Succubus | **Charm Boost** Skill Needed for quest | N/A |
>
> You will need to dismiss Succubus first before you can fuse it.

Hand in quest 22 to Elizabeth, which unlocks:

- 23\. Fusion Series #3 Devil, Succubus

You'll be able to hand in 23 straight away, this unlocks:

- 24\. Fusion Series #4: Hierophant, Daisoujou

Summon back Jatayu.

**Ptolomea B8**

Persona 3 Reload, Ptolomea B9-B17, Hard - YouTube

Tap to unmute

[Persona 3 Reload, Ptolomea B9-B17, Hard](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ox6446uIRlY) [Ten Square](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCCmfMTH_CHvv6AoAFqDMUqQ)

![thumbnail-image](https://yt3.ggpht.com/ytc/AIdro_ko8Nruzehk1fzJLm9GrGV0KF80I7AJTCmAAYWeQQ=s68-c-k-c0x00ffffff-no-rj)

Ten Square191 subscribers

[Watch on](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ox6446uIRlY)

When you're ready, head for the door called Ptolomea B8 and enter. Continue downwards until you reach B17 (Teleporter is on B16) for the next gatekeeper, after defeating it you'll will get the Hermit Major Arcana. Head to your right and open the chests, then give the Hero's Cape to Ken. Head for the door to return to the Desert of Doors.

Speak to Elizabeth, she will have a new quest for you:

- 39\. Bring me an Emperor's Horn

You might be able to hand this in straight away, as Emperor's Horn drops from the mobs behind the Monad Doors.

Return to the dorm and Fuuka will install more parts on Aigis, further enhancing Ambush. This one is a new skill for Episode Aigis, you'll automatically break objects when you run into them.

Cook with Junpei

- Characteristics Unlocked: Critical Amp, greatly raises critical rate and greatly increases critical hit strength

(Increases crit rate by 10% and critical hit damage by 40%)

Head to the 2F and read with Yukari

- Characteristics Unlocked: Healing Apex

(Reduces the SP cost of recovery skills by 75%)

Speak to the Flower Shop Lady in Paulownia Mall to receive your harvest, then plant whatever you like.

Sell your loot at the police station, you can set the difficulty to peaceful to get the most value from your loot.

Farm until you have 160-190k yen.

Enter the Velvet room and register your personas.

> Depending on the level of your personas, you might not be able to summon these yet, if you can't keep using what you currently have until you can.

If you carried over the **DLC Fusion Guide:**

Summon **Titania\***, and **Vanadis**

Dismiss **Goemon** and **Kaguya**

If you carried over the **Non DLC Fusion Guide**

Summon **Titania\*** and **Chernobog** Dismiss **Black Frost** and **Succubus**

**\*** Summon Titania from the carried over compendium, not the one you recently fused.

**Ptolomea B17**

Persona 3 Reload, Ptolomea B18-B25, Hard - YouTube

Tap to unmute

[Persona 3 Reload, Ptolomea B18-B25, Hard](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WXyE_vx7OkI) [Ten Square](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCCmfMTH_CHvv6AoAFqDMUqQ)

![thumbnail-image](https://yt3.ggpht.com/ytc/AIdro_ko8Nruzehk1fzJLm9GrGV0KF80I7AJTCmAAYWeQQ=s68-c-k-c0x00ffffff-no-rj)

Ten Square191 subscribers

[Watch on](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WXyE_vx7OkI)

When you're ready, head for the door called Ptolomea B17 and enter. Continue downwards until you reach B25 and enter the door, after the cutscene you'll unlock:

- Achievement/Trophy: The Selfless One (53/56)
- 2x Random Party member's second Theurgies

Once you're back in the dorm, head up to the Landing and you'll find, 1x Twilight Fragment

> If you still need Golden Plates, Red Wall Fragment, Shackles of Madness or an Emperor's Horn, then return to Ptolomea and farm the Monad Doors until they drop. You can use Fuuka's Jamming ability so you can focus on the Monad Doors.
>
> If you still need a Gold Medal, I'd wait till Fuuka learns Tartarus Search at level 70. That way you can return to Ptolomea and get a guaranteed fight when it spawns. When you're able to hand in the Gold Medal quest, you'll get the last upgrade for Ambush, which will reduce the time it takes to activate.

Farm until you are at least level 64, it's the level you need to be, in order to do some fusions later.

Hand in quests 29, 33, 34, 38 and 39, which unlocks:

- 35\. Bring me White Sand
- 36\. Bring me a Throne of Mania

Head to Paulownia Mall and speak to the Flower Shop Lady to receive your harvest, then plant whatever you like. You can now buy Golden Tomato Sprout's.

Sell your loot at the police station, you can set the difficulty to peaceful to get the most value from your loot. You can also sell any gear you're not using.

Head to the antiques store and upgrade your gear. I highly recommend getting Aigis weapon Kyriotes, it has Auto Masuku on it which is Acc/Eva up for the whole party.

> Same as the main game, Growth 3 will be used to get personas to drop their heart items.

Grab a Growth 3 card, while you're at the antiques store, afterwards use the clock in the Desert of Doors to make a few copies.

> Before you decide on what gear you want to upgrade, equip any Anguish gear you got from the Monad Doors, it is very strong and can be better than the gear you can get from the antiques store. If you want to try farm more Treason gear, you can keep farming the Monad Doors in Cocytus.
>
> If you've been farming Soul Sea Drop's from Jatayu, you should have enough to get the Power Transmitter (Attack up for the whole party)
>
> Due to rng of base material drops (Prime Nihil Blade/Cloth/Ore) some of these items might not show up for you.
>
> Under Trade Items you have a pretty big selection of converting gems into other gems and general persona heart items, can make use of this if you are slightly short on specific gems.

If you brought over the **DLC Fusion Guide**:

**Decarabia** can be dismissed as you have **Anat** for Almighty

Persona 3 Reload, Judecca B1-B8, Hard - YouTube

> The Arena Fights can be longer, so I'd swap out Power Transmitter for a Ring of Eminence, ideally one that has All Stat +7 on it, if you don't have one, strength or magic +13 works well too.

When you're ready, head for the door called Colosseo Purgatorio and enter.

You'll have 3 separate fights, where you will go 2 on 2 against your former party members. After each fight you'll be sent back to the dorm, where you can save, and your HP and SP will be restored.

Once you've beaten all 3 fights, a new door will appear in the Desert of Doors. The rest of the team have now rejoined your party.

You can now get BiS gear for the rest of the party.

> BiS gear is highly optional, you can beat the final boss without it. If you do want to farm endgame gear you can use the same strategy as before to farm Nihil White Models, Nihil Black Models and Dead Moon's Husks
>
> Depending on how much gear you want to upgrade, you're going to need a lot of gems, especially Gold Quartz, Diamonds and Rubies. You can trade gems into other gems at the antiques store, but their doesn't seem to be a way to focus on specific gems. All the mob spawns are random, and all the chests that drop gems are random too, you can get the Devil Major Arcana card, which doubles the amount of drops you get. If I become aware of a better way to farm gems, I'll update the guide.
>
> Your best bet is just to run around the final section of Empyrean and fight all the mobs, do the Monad Doors, and open any gold chests you see. The final block will give you the best chance of getting Gold Quartz, Diamonds and Rubies, plus you'll also get other gemstones that you can later trade at the antiques store.
>
> If you want to finish of the Episode Aigis compendium you can do that too if you wish. You'll get a lot of yen while farming all the gemstones, so you can summon any personas you're missing from the carried over compendium. If you want to quickly see which personas you're missing, go to the Episode Aigis compendium and find one you're missing, then press RT/R2 to swap to the carried over compendium. It will still be on the same persona even if it's a different level, you can then summon it, to fill in that slot. This doesn't work for special fusion personas, or unique ones that don't show in the compendium, like Shiva.
>
> If you want the Armour of Light and Shoes of Light, you'll need to fuse Messiah to get the heart item, you can make him by fusing Thanatos and Orpheus.
>
> Kokuseki Senjin/Snow Queen Whip for Mitsuru, which one is better depends on what accessory you use. If you go for a Null accessory, then Snow Queen Whip is better as the 50% Ice Amp, is stronger than the 25% Magic Ability. If you choose to go for Varna Bracers (Ice Amp), then Kokuseki Senjin is better as the Magic Ability will stack with the Ice Amp.
