## Episode Aigis - Heartless Difficulty

### Episode Aigis - Heartless Part 2

**Ptolomea**

> Golden Plates and Red Wall Fragment drops from mobs behind the Monad Doors in this block, if you don't get them this time, you will have chances in upcoming blocks The Greedy Shadow drops the Gold Medal in this block, but catching it is a matter of luck. If you're having bad luck catching it, Fuuka eventually learns Tartarus Scan at level 70. You can then return to this block to get the Gold Medal.

When you're ready, head for the door called Ptolomea and enter. Make your way down to B8 (Teleporter is on B7) to find the next gatekeeper, after defeating it you'll get the Chariot Major Arcana. Head to your right and open the chests, then give Aristocracy to Mitsuru, before using the door to return to the Desert of Doors.

> You might be able to hand in quests 29, 33 and 34. If you managed to get a Gold Medal, Golden Plates or Red Wall Fragment.

Hand in quest 9 and 19 (Koumokuten) to Elizabeth, which unlocks:

- 10\. Bring me Circe's Bow
- 20\. Create a Persona with Auto Masuku
- 38\. Bring me Shackles of Madness

You might be able to hand in quest 38, Shackles of Madness also drops from the mobs behind the Monad Doors as well.

Return to the dorm for a cutscene, afterwards you will unlock:

- 2x Random party members second Theurgies

Read a book with Akihiko

- Characteristics Unlocked: Buff Amp, greatly increases the effects of buffs on self

(Increases the effects of buffs on self by 30%)

Head up to the 3F and spend time with Mitsuru

- Characteristics Unlocked: Ailment Surge, greatly raises critical rate on foes with ailments

(Raises party's critical rate by 15% on foes with ailments)

Head to Paulownia Mall and speak to the Flower Shop Lady, to receive your harvest, then plant whatever you like.

Sell your loot at the police station

Need to do a fusion for a quest, register your personas and then fuse:

| **Fusion Recipe** | **Skills to Pass on** | **Skills to Keep** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Narcissus and Jatayu<br>Result: Succubus | **Charm Boost** Skill Needed for quest | N/A |

Summon back Jatayu.

Hand in quest 23 to Elizabeth, which unlocks:

- 24\. Fusion Series #4: Hierophant, Daisoujou

Can dismiss Succubus

* * *

**Ptolomea B8**

> You'll want Koumokuten to learn Enduring Soul, before doing the next batch of fusions.

When you're ready, head for the door called Ptolomea B8 and enter. Continue downwards until you reach B17 (Teleporter is on B16) for the next gatekeeper, after defeating it you'll will get the Hermit Major Arcana. Head to your right and open the chests, then give the Hero's Cape to Ken. Head for the door to return to the Desert of Doors.

Speak to Elizabeth, she will have a new quest for you:

- 39\. Bring me an Emperor's Horn

You might be able to hand this in straight away, as Emperor's Horn drops from the mobs behind the Monad Doors.

Return to the dorm and Fuuka will install more parts on Aigis, further enhancing Ambush. This one is a new skill for Episode Aigis, you'll automatically break objects when you run into them.

Cook with Junpei

- Characteristics Unlocked: Critical Amp, greatly raises critical rate and greatly increases critical hit strength

(Increases crit rate by 10% and critical hit damage by 40%)

Head to the 2F and read with Yukari

- Characteristics Unlocked: Healing Apex

(Reduces the SP cost of recovery skills by 75%)

Speak to the Flower Shop Lady in Paulownia Mall to receive your harvest, then plant whatever you like.

Sell your loot at the police station.

You should be able to grab Kyriotes and the Power Transmitter from the antiques store, these are Auto Masuku and Auto Mataru, which is acc/eva and attack up for the whole party. Equip both of these on Aigis.

> Power Transmitter is the first of the two items you'll want to use your Soul Sea Drops on, the other item is later on and its Aigis's Pandemonium, which also requires 2x Soul Sea Drops.

Farm until, Koumokuten has learned Enduring Soul, you are at least level 65 and have at least 380k.

Save your game, enter the velvet room, register your personas and fuse:

The personas you should have on you are **Siegfried, Anat, Vanadis, Norn, Thor, Surt, Titania, Hell Biker, Barong and Daisoujou**

> You're starting to get into the endgame personas now, which all have unique Heart Items. You'll want to collect as many of these as you can, while you work your way through the dlc, that way you won't have to spend as much time farming them at the end.

Teach Growth 3 to Hell Biker, Norn, and Thor.

Hand in quest 56 to Elizabeth, which unlocks:

- 57\. Special Fusion Milestone #2

* * *

**Ptolomea B17**

When you're ready, head for the door called Ptolomea B17 and enter. Continue downwards until you reach B25 and enter the door, after the cutscene you'll unlock:

- Achievement/Trophy: The Selfless One (53/56)
- 2x Random Party member's second Theurgies

Once you're back in the dorm, head up to the Landing and you'll find, 1x Twilight Fragment

> If you still need Golden Plates, Red Wall Fragment, Shackles of Madness or an Emperor's Horn, then return to Ptolomea and farm the Monad Doors until they drop. You can use Fuuka's Jamming ability so you can focus on the Monad Doors.
>
> If you still need a Gold Medal, I'd wait till Fuuka learns Tartarus Search at level 70. That way you can return to Ptolomea and get a guaranteed fight when it spawns. When you're able to hand in the Gold Medal quest, you'll get the last upgrade for Ambush, which will reduce the time it takes to activate.

Hand in quests 29, 33, 34, 38 and 39, which unlocks:

- 35\. Bring me White Sand
- 36\. Bring me a Throne of Mania

Head to Paulownia Mall and speak to the Flower Shop Lady to receive your harvest, then plant whatever you like. You can now buy Golden Tomato Sprout's.

Sell your loot at the police station.

Head to the antiques store and upgrade your gear.

> Before you decide on what gear you want to upgrade, equip any Anguish gear you got from the Monad Doors, it is very strong and can be better than the gear you can get from the antiques store. If you want to try farm more Treason gear, you can keep farming the Monad Doors in Cocytus.
>
> If you've been farming Soul Sea Drop's from Jatayu, you should have enough to get the Power Transmitter (Attack up for the whole party)
>
> Due to rng of base material drops (Prime Nihil Blade/Cloth/Ore) some of these items might not show up for you.
>
> Under Trade Items you have a pretty big selection of converting gems into other gems and general persona heart items, can make use of this if you are slightly short on specific gems.

**Judecca**

When you're ready, head for the door called Judecca and enter. Make your way down to B8 (Teleporter is on B7) for the next gatekeeper, after defeating it you'll receive the Hierophant Major Arcana. Head to your right and open the chests and give the Circe's bow to Yukari, then head through the door to return to the Desert of Doors.

> You might be able to hand in quests 35 and 36, if you got a Throne of Mania or a White Sand from the mobs behind the Monad Doors.

Hand in quests 10 and 24 (Daisoujou, invigorate 3) to Elizabeth, which unlocks:

- 11\. Bring me a Pandemonium
- 25\. Fusion Series #5: Hierophant, Kohtyu
- 40, Bring me an Equal Balance

You might be able to hand in quest 40, as Equal Balance also drops from the mobs behind the Monad Doors.

Return to the dorm and watch a DVD with Fuuka

- Characteristics Unlocked: Weakness Mitigator, greatly reduces the damage taken when an ally's weakness is struck

(Reduces damage taken by 15% when an ally's weakness is struck)

Head to the 2F and spend time with Ken

- Characteristics Unlocked:

(Recover 10 SP each turn in battle)

Speak to the Flower Shop Lady to receive your harvest, then plant whatever you like.

Sell your loot at the police station.

* * *

**Judecca B8**

When you're ready, head for the door called Judecca B8 and enter. Continue downwards util you reach B17 (Teleporter is on B16) for the next gatekeeper, after defeating it you'll get the Fortune Major Arcana. Open the chests, then head for the door and return to the Desert of Time.

Hand in quest 20 (Barong, Auto Masuku), you'll also have a new quest:

- 41\. Bring me a Ruinous Rotary Leg

You might be able to hand in quests 41 straight away, as it drops from mobs behind the Monad Doors.

Return to the dorm and spend time with Koromaru

- Characteristics Unlocked: Auto Masukunda

(Lowers Accuracy/Evasion of all foes for 3 turns)

Head up the 3F and spend time with Metis

- Characteristics Unlocked: Sister Amp

(Attack up by 40% when receiving a Shift from Aigis)

Go to the Flower Shop Lady to receive your harvest, then plant whatever you like, you can now plant up to 4 seeds.

Sell your loot at the police station.

Farm until you're at level 69 and have at least 480k.

Save your game, enter the velvet room, register your personas and fuse:

| **Fusion Recipe** | **Skills to Pass on** | **Skills to Keep** |
| --- | --- | --- |

> If you need any duplicate Heart Items, you'll need to re-fuse that persona and repeat this process to get the extra Heart Item.

The personas you want on you are **Satanael, Siegfried, Metatron, Beelzebub, Saturnus, Odin, Skadi** and **Norn.** These personas will take you through to the end of the game.

> You can make some stronger personas using the dlc ones, as they are the only personas that have access to the severe tier multi-target spells. This does take quite a bit of extra farming though, so its best done at the same time as when you're preparing for the super boss.

Return to B26, and head down to B27. You'll get a cutscene and afterwards you'll unlock:

- Achievement/Trophy: The One Who Dreamt (55/56)

You'll then find yourself in a boss battle, which you need to defeat. Afterwards you'll get some more cutscenes and eventually find yourself back in the dorm.

When you're ready to continue, speak to Metis, and you'll be taken to the arena. Before heading in though, turn around and use the door to return to the Desert of Doors.

Head to Paulownia Mall and speak to the Flower Shop Lady to receive your harvest, then plant whatever you like.

Sell your loot at the police station.

> It's completely optional if you want to farm the specific gear I recommend. A lot of it comes from the Monad Doors, so there can be quite a bit of rng in getting the right gear to drop, along with the right sub effect.
>
> You can beat the arena fights with different gear, but it will be easier using this gear. If you need to farm any of the Monad Doors, you'll be fine even with 2 party members, as you'll both be level 99 and have some very strong personas.

**Colosseo Purgatorio**

It's worth summoning Scathach as she has all 3 party wide auto abilities on her and using her as your starter persona.

When you're ready, head for the door called Colosseo Purgatorio and enter.

You'll have 3 separate fights, where you will go 2 on 2 against your former party members. After each fight you'll be sent back to the dorm, where you can save, and your HP and SP will be restored.

Once you've beaten all 3 fights, a new door will appear in the Desert of Doors. The rest of the team have now rejoined your party.

You can now get BiS gear, for the entire party.

> BiS gear is highly optional, you can beat the final boss without it. If you do want to farm endgame gear, you're either going to need White Nihil Models, Black Nihil Models, Dead Moon's Husk, Eminence or Heresy Gear, all of this though can be highly rng, you do have a couple of options though:
>
> 1. Farm the Monad Doors in the final section of Empyrean, you can get White Nihil Models, Black Nihil Models, Dead Moon's Husk, and Eminence gear. You'll also get a good amount of the gems needed to make the gear.
> 2. Run around the final section of Empyrean, until you find a striped chest, make a note of where it is, then use a teleporter to return to the Desert of Doors and save the game. You can then return to the striped chest and open it, if it's not the base material you want (White Nihil Models, Black Nihil Models, Dead Moon's Husk), you can reload the save and keep trying until you get the one you want.
>
> Depending on how much gear you want to upgrade, you're going to need a lot of gems, especially Gold Quartz, Diamonds and Rubies. You can trade gems into other gems at the antiques store, but their doesn't seem to be a way to focus on specific gems. All the mob spawns are random, and all the chests that drop gems are random too, you can get the Devil Major Arcana card, which doubles the amount of drops you get. If I become aware of a better way to farm gems, I'll update the guide.
>
> Kokuseki Senjin/Snow Queen Whip for Mitsuru, which one is better depends on what accessory you use. If you go for a Null accessory, then Snow Queen Whip is better as the 50% Ice Amp, is stronger than the 25% Magic Ability. If you choose to go for Varna Bracers (Ice Amp), then Kokuseki Senjin is better as the Magic Ability will stack with the Ice Amp.

> If you're planning on taking on the super boss, you're going to need to do a lot of farming anyway, as you need a lot of gemstones to max out your strength, magic and endurance on all your party members that you plan to use in the fight. You also need to farm the Fortune Arcana cards to increase your hp. While you're doing this you are naturally going to accumulate a lot of base materials, due to how much farming you need to.
>
> Your best bet is just to run around the final section of Empyrean and fight all the mobs, do the Monad Doors, and open any gold chests you see. The final block will give you the best chance of getting Gold Quartz, Diamonds and Rubies, plus you'll also get other gemstones that you can later trade at the antiques store.
>
> The game likes to give you Major Arcana cards that you haven't gotten in a while, so I don't think abusing save scum is particularly effective for farming Fortune cards. Instead, just grab any Major Arcana card you get and keep going until you max out your stock. If this happens return to the dorm to reset the Arcana Burst, this time you should have a high chance of getting the Fortune card. As soon as you get it, return to the dorm to reset the burst and keep continuing until you get your hp where you need it to be.
