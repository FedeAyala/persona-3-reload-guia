## Main Game

### Epilogue

**Epilogue**

**March**

**3/4 Thursday**

**After School**

You get to catch up with all your party members and social links, they are scattered all over the place starting with Classroom2-F you have:

**Classroom 2-F**

- Junpei
- Kenji
- Yukari

**School Building 2F**

- Yuko
- Mr. Ono (Temperence)
- Fuuka
- Chihiro

**School Building 1F**

- Faculty Office
- Mitsuru
- Track Team Advisor (Star)
- Kazushi
- Hidetoshi
- Keisuke
- Akihiko

**School Entranceway**

- Ken

**Paulownia Mall**

- Bartender (Tower)

**Naganaki Shrine**

- Familiar Man (Hanged)
- Middle-Aged Lady (Sun)

**Iwatodai Strip Mall 1F**

- Gourmet Magazine Reporter (Moon)
- Bunkichi and Mitsuko

**Dorm Entrance**

- Koromaru

When you’ve finished talking to everyone, head inside the dorm.

**Evening**

- TV report (Devil)

Talk to Yukari by the shared computer, then look at the Scratched Photo on the desk.

Head to your room, after the text message go to sleep.

**3/5 Friday**

That’s it, all that's left to do is enjoy the final round of cutscenes. If you beat the game on merciless you will get a very small additional scene after the credits. After the credits you will unlock:

- Achievement/Trophy: From Shadows into Light (48/48)
