## DLC Fusion Guide (Main Game)

Welcome to the DLC fusion guide, this guide is designed to help you build well balanced personas, while also making sure you are getting as much use as possible out of those powerful DLC personas. This guide was designed with as little rng as possible, which means you won't have to farm any skill cards to make this fusion guide work, everything is passed on through fusion. I do recommend some optional skill cards from time to time, but they are just usually for a bit of extra damage or a higher crit rate. The DLC fusion guide doesn't start until July, that's because during the opening months of the game you are too low level to be able to fuse them, during these months the fusion guide is the same for both DLC and non DLC personas.

These are some of the endgame personas you'll be working towards over the course of the game. Stats may vary, depending on how many and what incenses you use during the course of the game.

> You’ve likely noticed that this fusion guide is heavily focused on passing on the auto skills, and a SP regen skill. There are a few reasons for this, firstly they are great skills, allowing you to do more damage, take less damage, be more accurate and help manage your SP. For a good 95% of fights these skills won't even expire and having them on all your personas means you don’t have to constantly switch to a specific persona at the end of every fight, allowing for a smoother gameplay experience. It also makes doing this fusion guide a lot simpler as it focuses on a handful of skills, with slight updates over time. When you get to the end game personas though, you'll be making a few more specialized ones, there are a lot of strong skills at endgame and there just aren't enough slots to do auto skills and these end game skills on the same persona.
